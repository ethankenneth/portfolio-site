Deadlier Than Sharks

Sharks are demonized in the media, but yet, when compared to some pretty boring everyday things, they are not really that dangerous. Find out some of the startling statistics about common things that are more likely to kill you than a shark is. To enable, just say "Alexa, enable Deadlier Than Sharks," or click "Enable." Then, say "Alexa, ask Deadlier Than Sharks a fact."

Christmas Movies

Sure, everyone knows Elf and A Christmas Story, but here's a list of some overlooked and underrated Christmas movies you may never have heard of! To enable, just say "Alexa enable Christmas Movies." To use, simply say "Alexa, open Christmas Movies," or "Alexa, ask Christmas Movies to give me a fact."

Meat Facts

Love meat? Beef? Bacon? Chicken? How about dairy? Milk? Eggs? To enable, simply click "Enable Skill," or say "Alexa, enable the Meat Facts skill." Then, say "Alexa, ask Meat Facts for a fact."
